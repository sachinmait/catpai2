About the Program

The CATPAI Executive Program is a 6-week online course designed to help professionals and graduates upskill in AI. With live sessions, hands-on projects, and expert guidance, you'll gain the knowledge and certification to excel in the AI-driven world.

Program Details

  • Duration: 6 Weeks (Every Saturday)
  • Session Timing: 10 AM to 1 PM
  • Delivery Mode: Online (Live)
  • Program Fee: 15,000 INR + GST
  • Eligibility: Open to professionals & graduates

Program Modules

Module 1: Artificial Intelligence - Global Game Changer

Explore the impact of AI on various industries and learn about generative AI applications.

Module 2: Introduction to Large Language Models

Understand LLMs like ChatGPT, Gemini, and CoPilot, and master prompt engineering.

Module 3: Applicability of Generative AI in Business

Learn how AI can automate customer service, enhance marketing, and optimize business processes.

Module 4: AI in Creative Arts, Media, and Education

Discover AI-powered tools for design, video production, and education.

Module 5: Customizing Your Work with AI

Integrate AI into existing systems and develop custom AI solutions for your business.

Module 6: Ethical & Legal Implications of AI

Address AI bias, intellectual property rights, and regulatory compliance.
